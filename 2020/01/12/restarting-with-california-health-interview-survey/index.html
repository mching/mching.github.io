Restarting with California Health Interview Survey

 Posted on January 12, 2020  |  Mike Ching

Lately this blog has had a lot of descriptive posts about my life but not much that stretches my R abilities. Later this year my organization is holding its research day, so I decided to start working on a dataset that I last used about 7 years ago. The California Health Interview Survey is a survey of households that is weighted to be representative of the non-institutionalized population of California.
